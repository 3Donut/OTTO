void otto::engines::PotionSynth::PanSM::pos ( float  v)

Set position (constant power law)

This is a constant power pan where the sum of the squares of the two channel gains is always 1. A quadratic approximation is used to avoid expensive trig function calls. The approximation is good enough for most purposes and gives the exact result at positions of -1, 0, 1.
